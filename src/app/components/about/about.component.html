Our Story

Curry Palace was born from a dream to share the rich culinary heritage of India with the vibrant city of Melbourne. Founded in 2016 by Chef Rajesh Kumar, our restaurant has become a beloved destination for authentic Indian cuisine.

What started as a small family restaurant has grown into a cornerstone of Melbourne's dining scene, serving traditional recipes passed down through generations. Every dish tells a story of our homeland, prepared with love and the finest ingredients.
